Remote Cabin [PS4]

Remote Cabin is a player home and settlement just north of Vault 111. Exit vault, turn around, walk north.

Note that version 15 introduces a new trigger inside that cabin that enables the Workshop's "Build Areas". This was left out of earlier versions for testing purposes. If you came from an earlier version, the build menu should be available as normal after entering the main cabin.

Status:

  • Work-in-Progress
  • Building components cheat box
  • Spawn settler button
  • 80% navmesh refactored
  • Experimental Booze Workbench

Features:

  • Locked door puzzle

  • Weapon, armor, grenade, ammo storage containers

Craftable consumables:

  • All booze from vanilla game
  • All "Ice Cold" versions of booze from vanilla game
  • Old Vaquero
  • Whiskey Rose
  • White Russian
  • Moe's Mouthwash
  • 3 more exclusive Dunwich Elixirs!

Special thanks to Serpentine90210 for helping develop and test most of the drinks!

Craftable workshop items:

  • Custom workbenches
  • Booze related clutter
  • Holiday decorations
  • Dog and Cat rugs
  • Dog furniture (ask your Dog to investigate)
  • Guard chairs
  • Partying mats
  • Shop chairs

Remember to craft Blueprints at the Booze Bench and hold the ones you want to show up in your build menu. Blueprint storage containers have been provided.
